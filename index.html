    Anti-Lock Braking

    No more skidding and loss of steering on ice, with anti-lock braking on the front tire, you can steer yourself back on track even in the icy-est conditions!

    Traction Control

    A high-speed control system is always monitoring each wheel, making sure that power is delivered to the right tire at the right time. This allows you to start your bike on ice without losing control, and makes sure that you can turn seamlessly.

    Heating

    Built-in heating for your hands keeps your fingers warm even in extreme cold, and heating for the battery makes sure that you get the range you expect -- no matter what the conditions.

    Lighting

    Super bright LED lighting allows you to see and be seen. No more riding at night finding that your clip-on light's battery has died either - the power for the lights comes directly from the main battery and your pedalling power!

    Drop-In Frame Design

    The design of the aluminimum rear frame means it can be installed on any conventional 26" bike frame. You can easily turn an un-wanted and un-safe winter junker into the cream of the crop in biking technology!

    Series Hybrid Powertrain

    Our series-hybrid design uses both battery and pedalling to power through snow and provide a natural and adjustable riding experience. The design allow you to always pedal at your optimal cadence and torque, leaving the hard work of gear-shifting behind.

    In addition, getting rid of a long chain helps for cold weather performance and corrosion resistance, allowing you to confidently ride through the snow and salt of Canadian (or other) roads.
